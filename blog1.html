The Adventure of Odysseus: Characters

Date Characters

    Odysseus - a hero who manages to pass through obstacles to get to his family
    Penelope - the wife of Odysseus
    Telemachus - the son of Odysseus
    Athena - a goddess of wisdom and war who decided to punish the Greeks but since she liked Odysseus and decided to help him without Poseidon knowing
    King Alcinuos - the king of the Phaecians
    Calypso - a woman who took care for Odysseus ever since Odyessus crashed his ship there
