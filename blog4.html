The Adventure of Odysseus: Essential Question

Date Summaries

    What is the relation with Greek Gods, heroes, and humans to real life?
  • Response - From this story we can see that mythological stories teach lessons to the people in the present. This story teaches many lessons, one of which is to be selfless or to not be greedy. An example of this is when the crew killed the oxen of the sun which led to the crew dying to the sun. This part of the story also teaches self restraint. However, the most prevalent lesson this story teaches is shown by Telemachus and Penelope. Even though years passed they endured through the hardships that the suitors had put them through, teaching the lesson of perseverance. It is also shown by how Odysseus traveled for 20 years. Both of these lessons can be applied to present day life
